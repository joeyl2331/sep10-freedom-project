Hardware Tool

The first 3D printer was produced by Chuck Hull in 1987. It took Hull 4 years to build it. 3D printers have been very popular in the fashion industry because it has many uses. Iris Van Herpen had used it to create a 3D printed dress which had gotten a lot of praise from people. The 3D printer is sustainable because it can be used to make small accessories without wasting resources. It also is helpful for designers to build their prototypes and take more design risks before making the final change for their products.
The FX mirror (virtual fitting room) was launched on November 15, 2018. It was founded by the FX Gear CEO Kwang-Jin Choi. This mirror is easy and convenient to use. First you would need to stand in front of the mirror and make a pose to determine your size. Then you can browse through the different clothing options by using hand gestures or using the tablet connected to the mirror. You can also move around to see how the clothing will look on you from different angles.
The Atria Digital Cutter was launched in October 2020. It is designed by Lenny Marano. The cutter has many advantages because it can increase the throughput by 50%, reduces material usage by 15%, it makes zero buffer cuts ( Perfect and precise cuttings), and overall it saves more time and money.



Future Inventions

An invention that I have in mind is the four in one machine. It contains a scanner, a touchscreen desktop, a 3D printer, and a cutter that makes clothes. The scanner scans the design draft and on the touchscreen desktop, you can edit more in depth of the draft and on how you want your design to look like. For the desktop, there are tools provided digitally. With the desktop, it comes with a stylus pen and an app for the 3D printer. The cutter produces the final product of your design. There are also two slots connected to the ends of the cutter. For one of the slots, you import the fabric that you want to use for your product. The other slot is where the finished product comes out.
