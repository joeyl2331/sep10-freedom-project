Hardware Tools

The first 3D printer was produced by Chuck Hull in 1987. It took Hull 4 years to build it. 3D printers have been very popular in the fashion industry because it has many uses. Iris Van Herpen had used it to create a 3D printed dress which had gotten a lot of praise from people. The 3D printer is sustainable because it can be used to make small accessories without wasting resources. It also is helpful for designers to build their prototypes and take more design risks before making the final change for their products.

Future Inventions

An invention that I have in mind is the four in one machine. It contains a scanner, a touchscreen desktop, a 3D printer, and a cutter that makes clothes. The scanner scans the design draft and on the touchscreen desktop, you can edit more in depth of the draft and on how you want your design to look like. For the desktop, there are tools provided digitally (A ruler, pen, pencil). With the desktop, it comes with a stylus pen and an app for the 3D printer. On the app, you can pick out the colors before you make your 3D product, you can also adjust the size of your 3D product. The 3D printer makes a 3D version of your design. The cutter produces the final product of your design. There are also two slots connected to the ends of the cutter. For one of the slots, you import the fabric that you want to use for your product. The other slot is where the finished product comes out.
